About Me

Filipino - current location in Singapore.

I started my professional career in an Australian IT-solution company in the Philippines. The company specializes on banking softwares for the retail consumers in Australia and UK. I did programming and have used:

  • Smalltalk, (an object oriented language developed by Alan Kay, the father of OOP);
  • Java (it was also called J2EE back then) for building web applications.
As a fresh graduate, I have not only learnt technical skills - but critical analysis and thinking outside the box.

After three years of experience, I left my homeland and accepted a job offer overseas. I worked on in-house applications of a French bank with customers across different continents. My experience there has taught me how to be agile, be adaptive, without sacrificing good architectural design and best development practices.

As a Technical lead, below are some of my proud achievements and learnings:

  • Migrated Monolithic applications into Microservices.
  • Successfully implemented CI/CD pipeline.
  • The test driven development and automation.
  • Migration into Cloud.
  • A lit bit of Project Management for Technical deliverables.

Fast forward to today, I have moved to a Telecommunication company in Singapore. What I enjoyed the most is learning the latest technologies not just bound to softwares. These are like gifts to me and by writing a technical blog, I hope that this will also help other people the way many technical blogs has helped me.

Let me know if you want me to cover any particular topic. :)
