Filipino - Software Engineer currently in Singapore.

I started my working career in an Australian IT-solution company in the Philippines. The company specilized on banking-solution applications for retail consumers. I did programming and have used

  • Smalltalk, (an object oriented language developed by Alan Kay, the father of OOP)
  • and Java (it was also called J2EE back then) for building web applications.
As a fresh graduate, I've learned not just technical skills - but I've also developed critical functional analysis and thinking outside the box.

After three years, I left home and accepted a job offer in Singapore. The employer is a French corporate bank and I was assigned to work on the core banking systems of the bank. My experience there has taught me on how to be agile and that business requirements are always changing. I was learning the latest Tech stack which I really enjoyed and I found that learning something new is what will make us grow and evolve in our career.
As a Technical lead, I'm proud to have experience the following:

  • Migrated Monolithic applications into Microservices.
  • Successfully implemented CI/CD pipeline.
  • The magic of Test driven development and the beauty of Cloud.
  • A lit bit of Project Management for Technical deliverables.

Fast forward to today, I currently work in Telecommunications industry in Singapore. Our mission is to be the best Telecommunication company in Asia. I am involved on system design and architecture of Network services and I currently lead the Test Strategy of an orchestration system for 5G. I'm learning new things related to 5G Technology and it's use cases and I can't wait to journal these too..
