About Me

Filipino - current location in Singapore.

I started my professional career in an Australian IT-solution company in the Philippines. The company specializes on banking softwares for the retail consumers in Australia and UK. I did programming and have used:

  • Smalltalk, (an object oriented language developed by Alan Kay, the father of OOP);
  • Java (it was also called J2EE back then) for building web applications.
As a fresh graduate, I have not only learnt technical skills - but business analysis and thinking outside the box.

After three years of experience, I left my homeland and accepted a job offer overseas. I worked on in-house banking applications of a French bank based in Singapore. My experience there has taught me agile development, SOLID principle concepts, and the 12 Factors app - all that I have brought with me.

As a Technical lead, below are some of my proud achievements and learnings:

  • Migrated Monolithic applications into Microservices.
  • Successfully implemented CI/CD pipeline.
  • The test driven development and automation.
  • Migration into Cloud.
  • A lit bit of Project Management for Technical deliverables.

Fast forward to today, I have moved to a Telecommunication company in Singapore. What I enjoyed the most is learning the latest technologies not just bound to softwares. Embracing the open source community, participating virtual events and technical forums are feeding my curiousity about the future. I'll be sharing these interest through technical blogging and hoping that I could also contribute to the open source community. :)

Let me know if you want me to cover any particular topic.
